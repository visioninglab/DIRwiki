Glossary

Terms, acronyms, and abbreviations used across the DIR Case Study Wiki.

AI
Artificial Intelligence — the simulation of human intelligence by computer systems, used in smart infrastructure, threat detection, and predictive maintenance.
AECOM
Global infrastructure consulting firm; joint venture partner overseeing Singapore DTSS Phase 2.
BBM / BM
Bus-Bike-Metro / Bus-Metro — multimodal transit network configurations studied in the China Urban Mobility case.
Black & Veatch
Engineering firm; joint venture partner with AECOM on Singapore DTSS Phase 2.
CCC
China Compulsory Certification — mandatory product safety certification for EV charging equipment, effective August 2026.
CDC
Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (USA) — provides the Social Vulnerability Index used in Broward County risk mapping.
CER
Critical Entities Resilience Directive (EU 2022/2557) — aims to strengthen resilience of critical entities against natural hazards, terrorist attacks, and sabotage.
COP28
28th Conference of the Parties to the UN Framework Convention on Climate Change, held in Dubai in 2023.
CRO
Chief Resilience Officer — a dedicated leadership role for coordinating resilience strategy, as in Broward County.
DIR
Design for Infrastructure Resilience — the research framework underlying this wiki.
DORA
Digital Operational Resilience Act — EU regulation for the financial sector’s digital resilience.
DT / Digital Twin
A virtual replica of a physical system that receives real-time data for monitoring, simulation, and predictive analytics.
DTSS
Deep Tunnel Sewerage System — Singapore’s 206 km underground used-water superhighway connecting to three water reclamation plants.
ENISA
European Union Agency for Cybersecurity — provides guidance and coordinates cyber resilience across EU member states.
EV
Electric Vehicle.
EVCIPA
Electric Vehicle Charging Infrastructure Promotion Alliance (China).
FEMA
Federal Emergency Management Agency (USA) — provides Community Resilience Disaster Zones data used in Broward County planning.
GIS
Geographic Information System — technology for capturing, storing, and analysing spatial and geographic data.
HDPE
High-Density Polyethylene — corrosion-resistant material used for tunnel linings in Singapore DTSS.
IDS
Intrusion Detection System — monitors network traffic for suspicious activity.
IoT
Internet of Things — network of physical devices embedded with sensors and connectivity for data exchange.
ISO/IEC 27001
International standard for information security management systems.
IWMF
Integrated Waste Management Facility — at Tuas, Singapore, integrating used water and solid waste treatment.
LANDSAT 8
NASA/USGS Earth observation satellite providing temperature and land-use data, used in Broward County risk mapping.
MIIT
Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (China).
NASA
National Aeronautics and Space Administration (USA).
NCRAS
National Climate Resilience and Adaptation Strategy (Australia).
NDRC
National Development and Reform Commission (China) — key agency driving EV charging infrastructure policy.
NEWater
Singapore’s brand of high-grade reclaimed water, purified from treated used water. Supplies approximately 55% of the nation’s water needs.
NIS2
Network and Information Security Directive 2 (EU 2022/2555) — EU-wide cybersecurity legislation replacing NIS1, with estimated implementation cost of €31.2B/year.
NIST
National Institute of Standards and Technology (USA) — provides cybersecurity frameworks referenced by EU directives.
PKI
Public Key Infrastructure — framework for managing digital certificates and encryption keys.
PUB
Public Utilities Board — Singapore’s National Water Agency, owner and operator of the DTSS.
SCADA
Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition — industrial control system for monitoring and controlling infrastructure processes.
SRB
Solid Rocket Booster — component of the Space Shuttle whose O-ring failure caused the Challenger disaster.
SVI
Social Vulnerability Index — CDC index measuring community vulnerability to health hazards, used in Broward County risk prioritisation.
TBM
Tunnel Boring Machine — heavy equipment used to excavate tunnels in Singapore DTSS construction.
UNESCO
United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization — designated the Dujiangyan Irrigation System as a World Heritage Site.
WRP
Water Reclamation Plant — facility for treating used water. Singapore’s three WRPs (Changi, Kranji, Tuas) are connected by the DTSS.
